People Matter

From the person filling your coffee in the morning to the folks stocking the shelves at your grocery store, thousands of working Americans are doing what they can to bring food to your table.

Delivery Drivers

The last mile is always the hardest...

...meaning, your box never magically arrives on your doorstep. It's a team effort of hundreds of people, but most importantly, the person who places the box at your doorstep. Drivers have to keep tight schedules, and sit in traffic for a long time every day. Ensure your parcel's safe delivery and feel good by giving a tip!

Wait Staff

Below Minimum Wage...

...that's the hourly pay of many waiters and waitresses. Even today, in 2019, restaurants pay waitstaff $3-4 less than the state's minimum hourly wage. Their work isn't worthless, and they deserve a living wage as well. Make sure your waiter can afford food, and reward anything extra by tipping superfluously!

20%

for good, average service
